IHM Maintenance Software
and Managed Services
€240 per vessel, per year

The EUSRR’s IHM guidance recommends using a software tool to keep maintenance up to date throughout the vessel’s lifecycle. Our easy to use cloud-based IHM maintenance software provides comprehensive reporting and tracking of supplier declarations of compliance, material declarations and location for all hazardous items onboard, and downloadable reports for inspections by port states and class societies.

All modules are fully customisable and can integrate with third parties for a unique solution

Our solutions include an IHM Maintenance Managed Service, where our HazMat team manage your IHM updates for you. We ensure that the correct documentation including SDoC and Material Declarations for items containing hazmat are uploaded, to maintain continuous conformity for your inventory.
